Lecture 6: Deployment & Media

This lecture covers adding media (images, videos) and deploying your website online so others can access it.

🎥 Lecture Video

📝 Lecture Notes

  • Adding images with <img>
  • Embedding videos with <iframe>
  • Media formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, MP4
  • Deployment options: GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel
  • Basic steps to upload your website online
